Team & Alumni

Our group brings together researchers from electrical engineering, neuroscience, materials science and medicine, united by a passion for translational biomedical technology.

Principal Investigator

Group Leader

👤

Prof. Andreas Demosthenous

Professor of Microelectronics · Department of Electronic & Electrical Engineering, UCL

Andreas Demosthenous leads the UCL Bioelectronics group, with research spanning analogue and mixed-signal integrated circuits for biomedical applications. His work covers implantable neural interfaces for Parkinson's disease and epilepsy, cochlear and retinal prostheses, electrical impedance tomography, and wireless power and data transfer for implanted devices.
