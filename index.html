What We Do

Research Areas

Our work spans four interconnected domains, united by the shared goal of improving human health through engineering innovation.

⚡

Sensing & Diagnostics

Wearable and implantable sensors for vital signs monitoring, electrical impedance spectroscopy, lab-on-a-chip for point-of-care diagnostics, and EIT for functional imaging.

Learn more →
🧠

Neural Interfaces

Fully implantable closed-loop systems for the central and peripheral nervous systems, treating Parkinson's, epilepsy, paralysis, hearing loss and more.

Learn more →
💊

Drug Delivery

Miniaturised intelligent drug delivery systems integrated with advanced microfabrication and MEMS technologies for targeted, controlled therapies.

Learn more →
📡

Energy & Communication

Energy harvesting, wireless powering and data communication strategies for next-generation autonomous implantable and wearable biomedical devices.

Learn more →
